In this code: - Each `.box` div represents an element with different positioning. - `.relative` is positioned relative to its normal position with a 20px offset from the top and left. - `.absolute` is positioned absolutely with a fixed position 50px from the top and left of the nearest positioned ancestor (or the initial containing block). - `.fixed` is positioned fixedly 10px from the top and right of the viewport. - `.sticky` is initially positioned relative to its normal position, but once it reaches the top of the viewport during scrolling, it sticks to the top.
